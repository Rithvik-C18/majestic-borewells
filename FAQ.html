Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our borewell drilling services and solutions.

What is the typical duration for borewell drilling? +

The duration varies depending on several factors:

  • Soil type and geological conditions
  • Required depth of the borewell
  • Location accessibility
  • Weather conditions

Generally, a standard borewell can be completed within 2-3 days, while more complex projects may take up to a week.

How do you determine the right location for drilling? +

We follow a comprehensive process to determine the optimal drilling location:

  • Geological survey of the area
  • Water table analysis
  • Soil testing
  • Distance from existing structures
  • Accessibility for drilling equipment

Our experts use advanced technology and years of experience to ensure the best possible location for your borewell.

What is your pricing structure? +

Our pricing is based on several factors:

  • Depth of the borewell
  • Type of soil and rock formation
  • Additional services required
  • Location and accessibility

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide maintenance services? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance services including:

  • Regular cleaning and maintenance
  • Water quality testing
  • Pump servicing and repairs
  • 24/7 emergency support

We recommend annual maintenance to ensure optimal performance and longevity of your borewell.
